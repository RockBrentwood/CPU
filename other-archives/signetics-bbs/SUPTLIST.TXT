80C51 Development Support Contacts

updated June 11, 1993

This list includes only sources for which recent advertisments or other commercial 
references could be found, and companies with whom we have had contact recently. The list 
is undoubtedly not complete, however no products have been left out intentionally for any 
reason other than for lack of information. Additional references will be added as they are 
found. 

The author of this list is not directly familiar with many of these products or companies 
and their presence in the list should not be considered any kind of endorsement. This is 
simply a summary list of what is being advertised or otherwise being promoted in the 
industry.

The first part of this document lists types of products and the seller or manufacturer 
name, along with a brief description. The second section contains the addresses, telephone 
numbers, and fax numbers (when available) for the sources in section one.
